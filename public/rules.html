Official Rules

Go to the Game

Heaven and hell are engaged in a never-ending struggle for souls. You represent one of these two factions. The board consists of 6 columns numbered from 2 to 8, representing different scenarios of the fight.

Heaven zone Hell zone Soul Shadow

The dark blue squares represent heaven, and the dark red squares represent hell. In each column, there is a green meeple representing the soul and a grey meeple representing its shadow, which is the good or evil disposition of that soul.

When you move a shadow, it is said that you have activated the corresponding column. In each turn, you can only activate two columns.

Start your turn as a representative of heaven:

  • Roll the dice.
  • Choose an option if possible. The shadows will move to their new circles.
  • Decide whether to stand or continue.

If you continue, go back to step 1. If you pass, it becomes your opponent's turn. Passing is important because, at the moment of passing, the souls are reunited with their shadows, and real progress occurs according to your objectives.

If you don't pass in time, the shadows that have advanced will move back to the soul's position, and you lose the turn.

The game continues until one player has three or more souls in their domain.
